Rooms

Moti Mahal

Step into the grandeur of Moti Mahal, where royal luxury meets modern comfort. This room is adorned with traditional decor, offering a serene escape for those looking to immerse themselves in history.

Mor Mahal

Symbolizing elegance, Mor Mahal offers breathtaking views of peacocks from its balcony. Guests can enjoy the tranquil beauty of the surrounding garden, where peacocks roam freely, enhancing your experience of luxury.

Chandra Mahal

Chandra Mahal combines exquisite decor with spacious interiors, making it a perfect retreat for families. Enjoy moments of togetherness in this beautifully appointed room that reflects the essence of royal Rajasthan.

Hawa Mahal

Experience the charm of Hawa Mahal, where heritage meets comfort. With its breezy ambiance and stunning views, this room reflects the essence of Rajasthan's majestic history.
