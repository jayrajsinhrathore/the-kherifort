About Us

Our History

The Kheri Fort is a fort situated near Jaipur city in the Indian state of Rajasthan. Built by Thakur Budh Singh in the 18th century, it is approximately 45 kilometers from Jaipur city. The fort features a majestic entrance gate and beautifully built ramp stairs linking the garden area and the entrance door. It serves as a residence for a royal family and part of it has been converted into a tourist spot, allowing visitors to enjoy vintage jeep rides and horse riding.
